Disclaimer

We give our best to keep the data updated, but it is a very difficult task. For current rules in a country, please check their official sites.
Also avoid making simple comparisons - just implementing or abolish a measure is not the single reason to influence the number of infections.

An example for a scientific research is The estimated impact of non-pharmaceutical interventions ondocumented cases of COVID-19: A cross-country analysis, a study from Switzerland.

Always look at the bigger picture. But it this site might be helpful as a starter to dig into a research.

And if you see a mistake - please let us know! We need you, as a local or why every you know what you know (we love sources!)!
