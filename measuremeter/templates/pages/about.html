About/Info

Disclaimer

Disclaimer: With the reopenings it became nearly impossible to track the countries, as most switched to local measures.
Most of the country measures are not up-to-date.

With the regionalization, I now focus more on Switzerland, and keep the measures there always up to date.

Data like cases, deaths and positive rate are updated automatically and still valid.

For current rules in a country, please check their official sites.
Avoid making simple comparisons - just implementing or abolish a measure is not the single reason to influence the number of infections.

An example for a scientific research is The estimated impact of non-pharmaceutical interventions ondocumented cases of COVID-19: A cross-country analysis, a study from Switzerland.

Always look at the bigger picture. But it this site might be helpful as a starter to dig into a research.

And if you see a mistake - please let me know! We need you, as a local or why every you know what you know (we love sources!)!

Who am I

I am a random dude on the internet, without any professional expertise in medicine or statistics.
I just needed a project during the lockdown to keep me sane, as all my hobbies were forbidden in that time.
Because I love data, statistics and politics I started this site. I was confused by the different claims
what each country does as a reaction to Covid-19, so I wanted to have this information in one place.
I make changes because I feel I need it presented in another way - simply because I want. Or I want to try a new technology.

Give me the data

For Swiss measures, use this endpoint:
here
You can use these filters:

  • cantons: cantons (short form like zh for Zurich, ch for measures on national level)
  • types: measure types (see here for the list with ids
  • start: only show measures who started after this date (iso date)
  • end: only show measures who ended before this date (iso date)
Example: all mask measures from Geneva and Zurich, starting from August 8:
measuremeterdata/chmeasures/?cantons=ge,zh&types=2&start=2020-08-08
