Risk yesterday

Calculating the chance that one or more members of a group is/are infectious. Based on the model of the Georgia Institute of Technology.
Base number: Incidence of past 14 days per 100k population. CDC recommends 10 days.

Formula: 100 - (((1-(1/(100000/(cases_past14days*bias)))) ** groupsize) * 100)
